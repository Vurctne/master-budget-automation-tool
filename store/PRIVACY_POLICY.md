# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-07

Master Budget Automation Tool processes files that you choose on your local device.

## What the app does

- Reads local CSV, XLSX, or XLSM files that you select
- Writes a new local Excel workbook to the output path that you select
- Optionally uses local Microsoft Excel automation on Windows, when Microsoft Excel is installed, to preserve workbook features more accurately

## What the app does not do

- It does not require account sign-in
- It does not transmit your workbook data to an online service
- It does not collect analytics or telemetry
- It does not sell personal information

## Data storage

The app stores generated output workbooks and import report files only at locations chosen by the user.
